Spaced repetition:
- Review notes shortly after learning, then at increasing intervals (1 day, 3 days, 7 days, 14 days).
Active recall:
- Test yourself with questions and past papers instead of re-reading.
Past papers:
- Do timed practice. Mark honestly, list weak topics, then drill them.
Revision plan:
- Break modules into topics.
- Allocate sessions: (learn -> recall -> past paper -> review mistakes).
